This demo showcases the watermark, statistics, and related/recommendation features.

You can use the buttons to toggle between two different watermarks. When a video is finished playing a pop-up message displaying some statistics will be displayed. Finally when the video finishes, a list of related videos will be displayed. You can then click on the title of the video and it will load.
